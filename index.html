FAQ

What is Child Of The Dice?

Child Of The Dice is a distinctive, stylistically curated digital collection with a total of 999 pieces to be minted. Each portrait is drawn manually with randomly generated and unique traits.

The goal behind Child Of The Dice is to create an friendly, open and inclusive community centered around NFT artwork. Through charity events and the eclectic community, Child Of The Dice will be able to help children in need. The need to advocate for children's illness has become more apparent in recent years and thus, Child Of The Dice was born.

Who is the creator of Child Of The Dice?

The maximum supply of COTD will be 999.

Do you have a rarity system?

Yes, rarity is a very important aspect for this collection. Each piece is created manually and diversified with a specific rarity.

There are 5 types of rarities available ranging from ‘Classic’ to ‘Mythical'.

Where can I buy or sell my COTDs?

After mint, a marketplace will be available on DigitalEyes. Stay close for announcements and information on other marketplace listings as soon as they are available.

Do I own the COTD after purchasing ?

Yes, after purchase full intellectual properties belong to the buyer. More information will be available on the COTD website.

What does "COTD" mean?

COTD is the abbreviation for Child Of The Dice.
